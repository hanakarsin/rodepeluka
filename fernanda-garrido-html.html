Fernanda Villalobos - Age, Family, Bio

Best known by her social media handle of iamferv, she is both an Instagram and TikTok sensation who racked up more than 28 million fans on TikTok. In late 2018, she released her first original song titled "Perdemos el Control." In the summer of 2019, she released her second single titled "Solo a Mí." 

Before Fame

She began to establish herself online in 2017. Her first Instagram post was a video of her singing posted in March of that year. 

Trivia

Multiple posts on her Instagram account have been sponsored by the feminine hygiene brand Always. She has posted multiple fashion photos on her account displaying various outfits occasionally in front of cars and motorcycles. She has over 8 million followers on the platform. 

Family Life

She is from Chile. Her full name is Fernanda Valentina Villalobos Cortés. In 2020, was in a relationship with Kevlex Pazmiño. She previously dated Instagram star Martin Rompeltien. 

Associated With

In 2019, she was part of a panel on the Chilean show Cadena Nacional alongside Max Valenzuela and Ignacia Antonia. She's collaborated on TikTok with Kevlex Pazmiño.
