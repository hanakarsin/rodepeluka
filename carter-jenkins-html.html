Carter Jenkins - Age, Family, Bio

Actor who has appeared on many hit shows including the lead role of Surface, and guest roles on The Bernie Mac Show and Scrubs. He landed a starring role in the Freeform series Famous in Love.

Before Fame

He got his first experience working on a popular TV show when he had a minor role in Without a Trace in 2003.

Trivia

He had a role in the 2009 film Arcadia Lost, playing the role of Sye.

Family Life

His older brother, Renneker Jenkins, is a TV actor who has appeared on Dexter. He has dated model Sierra Swartz, who has appeared on his Instagram.

Associated With

He starred in the 2010 romantic comedy Valentine's Day and played the boyfriend of Emma Roberts.
