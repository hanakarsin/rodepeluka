Buhari Meets Ghana President, Addo-Akufo

President Muhammadu Buhari on Thursday met with his Ghanaian counterpart, Nana Addo-Akufo.

Naija News reports that the meeting was held behind closed-door at the Presidential Villa,  Abuja.

As at the time of filing this report, the agenda of the meeting between Buhari and Addo-Akufo who is the current chairman of ECOWAS has not been made public.

Recall that Buhari and Addo-Akufo had also met on Feb. 22 at the Presidential Villa, Abuja.

This meeting is coming 24-hours before the Super Eagles of Nigeria tackle the Black Stars of Ghana for a spot in the 2022 FIFA World Cup holding in Qatar.
