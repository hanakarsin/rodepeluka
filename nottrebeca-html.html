nottrebeca - Age, Family, Bio

TikTok sensation known for posting dance, trend and lip-syncing videos. Her account has gained over 2.3 million followers and more than 65 million likes. She has been a partner with the Wink social app and has advertised for it on her TikTok account. 

Before Fame

She posted her first TikTok in June 2020, gaining over 600,000 views and more than 100,000 likes.

Trivia

In January 2021, she posted a lip-syncing video that gained over 6 million views and more than 1 million likes. She dressed up as Lara Croft for Halloween in 2021 and displayed her costume in a post on her Instagram that has received over 141 thousand hearts. 

Family Life

She was born and raised in Aracaju, Sergipe, Brazil. She sometimes posts TikToks featuring her dog Lilo. She also rescued a cat named Mustache in February 2021.

Associated With

She often posts TikToks using audios from popular musicians, including Playboi Carti and Gotye.
