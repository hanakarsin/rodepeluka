OAP Toolz Reacts To Juicy Plastic Surgery Offer

Nigerian On-Air Personality, Tolulope Oniru, popularly known as Toolz,  has turned down the offer to go under the knife.

The voluptuous talk show host turned down a Brazilian Butt Lift (BBL) offer after a concerned fan on social media advised her to go through with the surgery to get a more desired body shape.

Stunned by the advice, Toolz turned the offer down, while wondering what she would look like if she goes ahead to add an extra bum to what she already had.

“Not yet… Wonder what I would look like if I did it,” the mother of two replied

The media personality is best known for hosting The Midday show on the beat 99.9 FM.

Nigerian Lady Reportedly Dies During Plastic Surgery

Meanwhile, a 28-year-old Nigerian lady identified as Amelia Pounds has reportedly died, following complications during liposuction surgery in India.

Naija News gathered that the businesswoman died during the surgery at an undisclosed hospital in New Delhi on Friday morning, October 7, 2022.

The Nigerian lady was said to have given up the ghost while being operated on.

In a video making the rounds online, the doctor was heard apologizing to her.

Also, an Instagram user confirmed the incident to Kenyan socialite, Vera Sidika, stating that the lady attempted to join her friend who recently underwent surgery.

The post read: “Hello Vera. Hopefully, u are doing well now, I have a story to share that happened here in India. One Nigerian girl just goes do surgery and she passes out. And the girl was very okay with her shape but because her friend did surgery and she survived it she thought it will be the same for her now she died what a pity.”
