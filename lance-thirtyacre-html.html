Lance Thirtyacre - Age, Family, Bio

Older brother of Logan Thirtyacre, AKA SuperMarioLogan, who helps his brother by providing his voice for several characters on Logan's YouTube channel. He has his own self-titled YouTube channel and his "Flicking off logan" video has received over 63 thousand views. 

Before Fame

When Logan was three years old, Lance fractured Logan's skull with a baseball bat.

Trivia

He previously ran a YouTube channel called 19Lance93 in which Logan helped operate. It would later be renamed Lancy Thirtyacre to follow in his brother's footsteps. In October of 2021, he posted a photo of himself next to the New England Patriots plane. 

Family Life

He and Logan have a younger sister named Haleigh. Their parents divorced when he was four years old and his mom remarried to a man named Brian. His dad was a truck driver. He is engaged to a woman named Audrey.

Associated With

He is a friend of YouTuber Lovell Stanton.
