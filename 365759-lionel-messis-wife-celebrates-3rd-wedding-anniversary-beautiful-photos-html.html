Lionel Messi's wife celebrates their 3rd wedding anniversary with special message

- Lionel Messi wife has celebrated their third wedding anniversary on social media

- The 33-year-old and his heartthrob have been together since their childhood days in Rosario

- Messi scored his 700th career goal during Barcelona's 2-2 draw with Atletico Madrid

Lionel Messi and Antonela Roccuzzo have continued to wax strong since their union as his wife celebrated their third wedding anniversary on her social media platforms.

Roccuzzo, who has been friends with the Barcelona star from her childhood shared her feelings with an Instagram clip which was viewed for more than 1.5million times within two hours of posting.

She wrote: "I will love you and you will love me, I love you and it is my only truth, And it is our great love, What will never die ..."

Antonela added: "Happy 3rd anniversary my love. I love you@leomessi" - complete with a heart emoticon.
