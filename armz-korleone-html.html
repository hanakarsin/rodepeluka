Armz Korleone - Age, Family, Bio

Bodybuilder and social influencer who has become known on Instagram creating workout and comedy videos for more than 500,000 followers. He also has a self-titled YouTube channel that features a variety of exercise tips and workout videos. He often shows appreciation for his fans, friends, and family.

Before Fame

He first began showcasing his workouts on YouTube in 2014. 

Trivia

Outside of bodybuilding, he is also a music aficionado and has a self-titled Soundcloud where he has released original music. He has been involved in boxing in the past. 

Family Life

His mother danced alongside him in a June 2017 Instagram video captioned "When mummy is more gassed than you..." 

Associated With

In June 2017, he trained with Simeon Panda and posted a video of the training on Instagram.
