    However, take note that this could also happen because of other reasons, such as poor network connection, so do not be too quick to jump into conclusions.

    Method 2: Deleting the contact

    Here is how to tell if someone blocked your number Android method:

  • Open the Contacts app on your Android device.
  • Tap on the contact name of the person you suspect may have blacklisted you.
  • Tap on the three vertical dots in the upper-right corner of your screen.
  • Tap "Delete" to delete their contact information.
  • Open your Contacts app again.
  • Tap on the search bar at the top of your screen and type in the person's name.

    Method 3: Using WhatsApp

    Here is how to know if someone blocked your number using WhatsApp:

  • Open your WhatsApp app on your mobile device.
  • Send a simple WhatsApp message to the person.
  • After you send the message, start watching for checkmarks next to the sent message:
    • The 1st checkmark will appear when the text leaves your phone.
    • The 2nd checkmark appears when the message is received on the other person's phone. If your text gets just one checkmark, then this means that the person has blacklisted you on WhatsApp, which is a reliable indicator that the person may have blacklisted you on their phone as well.
