Up-to-date list of national schools in Kenya as of 2024

National schools are always ranked as the most prestigious learning institutions and the top-most secondary schools in the country. They admit the best-performing learners from all 47 counties in Kenya. The list of national schools in Kenya contains nothing but the best institutes nationwide.

Initially, there were only 18 national schools in the country until 2011, when Sam Ongeri, who was the Minister of Education at the time, proposed the addition of 30 more schools. In 2013, the state was on a mission to promote social cohesion and national unity, thus elevating another 30 provincial schools into the national category. In 2014, another 25 institutes were added to the national level category, thus making the total number 103.

List of national schools in Kenya

In 2019, the total number of national secondary schools in Kenya was 105, but with the inclusion of special needs schools in this category, the number in 2020 has risen to 126. Since these institutes get to select only the best-performing KCPE candidates, they end up being the best national schools in Kenya.

What are the national schools in Kenya?

The national schools in Kenya are known for their high academic standards and great education programs. Examples of such schools include Alliance High School, Kenya High School, Starehe Boys' Centre and School and Moi Forces Academy, Eldoret.

Which national schools are the most selected in Kenya?

Some national schools in Kenya are considered highly competitive and are often among the most selected by top-performing students. They include Mangu High, Alliance Boys, Maseno Boys, Kenya High School, Limuru Girls High School, Starehe Girls Centre and Precious Blood Riruta.

How to choose a national school that fits you best?

Here are key factors to take into account when choosing the national school that suits you best.

  • Consider your academic goals and interests.
  • Think about your extracurricular interests and hobbies.
  • Consider your preferred location or city.
  • Determine your budget and financial considerations.
  • Research scholarship and financial aid opportunities.
  • Gather information on national schools in your country or region.

Is Machakos School a national school?

Machakos School is a boys national school located in Machakos County in Kenya. It was established in 1939 as Machakos School Institute.
