Betta Edu Approves Funds for Flight Tickets, Airport Taxis For Staff To Kogi

published on February 14, 2024
The Minister of Humanitarian Affairs and Poverty Alleviation, Betta Edu, approved funds for flight tickets and airport taxis for her ministry’s staff to Kogi State. Naija News reports that the minister gave the approval in a leaked internal memo dated November 6, 2023, which has now gone viral. The memo issued by the national programme manager, requesting the total sum of N72,374,500, was titled: ‘The Disbursement Under the 2023 Grant for Vulnerable Groups Programmes in Kogi State, 2023’, and was approved by the minister.
