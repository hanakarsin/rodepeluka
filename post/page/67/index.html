Buhari Arrives National Assembly For 2023 Budget Presentation

published on July 06, 2024
President Muhammadu Buhari has arrived at the National Assembly complex to present the proposed N19.76 trillion budget for the 2023 financial year. Naija News reports that the Nigerian leader was received into the complex by the Chairman of the National Assembly and the Senate President, Ahmad Lawan. The Speaker of the House of Representatives, Femi Gbajabiamila, was also on the ground to receive President Buhari to the National Assembly. President Buhari was accompanied by Vice President Yemi Osinbajo, Chief of Staff to President, Ibrahim Gambari; ministers, some presidential aides, and heads of government parastatals and agencies.
