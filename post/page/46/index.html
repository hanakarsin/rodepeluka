Nasarawa State Records 18,429 Hepatitis Positive Cases, Targets Treating 124,000 In 5Yrs

published on July 20, 2024
As the World marks Hepatitis day, Commissioner for Health in Nasarawa state, Ahmed Yahaya, yesterday Thursday, in Lafia said, the state has a record of 181, 946 persons who have tested positive for hepatitis infection. Mr. Yahaya said the Nasarawa state government is committed to eliminating viral hepatitis through the implementation of a five-year strategic plan with a target of treating 124,000 persons.
