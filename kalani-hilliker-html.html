Kalani Hilliker - Age, Family, Bio

Known for her appearances on Abby's Ultimate Dance Competition and Dance Moms beginning in season 4. She has also been featured on Shake It Up and the ABC Family original series Bunheads. In 2018, she began starring on the web series Dirt.

Before Fame

She first established herself in the public eye when she appeared on season 2 of AUDC, finishing in 4th place.

Trivia

She has won regional and national titles, including Junior Best Dancer at the Dance Awards NYC, Junior First Place at Nuvo Nationals, Junior First Place Jump Nationals, and National Teen Miss Showbiz.  She has over 7 million followers on Instagram.

Family Life

Her mother, Kira Girard, initially enrolled her in dance classes because she thought it would be fun to dress her daughter up. She has a brother named Jax, a half-brother named Jett, and a half-sister named Kaliah. She dated fellow dancer Brandon Chang.

Associated With

She is friends with both Mackenzie Ziegler and Madison Ziegler from Dance Moms.
