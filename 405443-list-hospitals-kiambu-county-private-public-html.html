List of hospitals in Kiambu County (private and public)

Are you looking for public or private hospitals in Kiambu County? Are you stranded which one to visit to get a specific treatment? Different hospitals in the county offer a range of services. Some are more equipped than others. To find the best one, you need to know important details about each facility.

Kiambu is one of the densely populated yet well-resourced counties in Kenya. It is easily accessible to constituents of other counties like Nairobi, Machakos and Nakuru. With tremendous efforts in health improvement initiatives, the county is home to some of the best hospitals in Kenya and East Africa. Below is a breakdown of the infirmaries.

Comprehensive list of hospitals in Kiambu County

Kiambu County is well-known for its diversity and convenience in accessing amenities. For this reason, private and public entities continue to set up several hospitals to curb the demand for quality healthcare in the region. They are as follows:

1. PCEA Kikuyu Hospital

The Presbyterian Church of East Africa (PCEA) Kikuyu Hospital is one of Kenya's oldest hospitals. It is also the major referral hospital in East Africa for its competent eye unit department. The hospital has an emergency department that operates 24 hours.

  • Location: Thogoto, Kikuyu
  • Contact 1: 0722 207 637 (General unit)
  • Contact 2: 0733 606 133 (Eye unit)
  • Contact 3: 0719 642 406 (Orthopedic unit)
  • Contact 4: 0786 546 457 (Dental unit)

2. AIC Kijabe Hospital

Another faith-based medical facility is the Kijabe Hospital, sponsored by the Africa Inland Church. It is a major referral centre in Kenya and has recently been classified as a level 6BHospital.

Likewise, the facility is an outpatient and inpatient clinic with a 365-bed capacity. Its attendants include both Kenyans and missionaries.

  • Location: Mathore, Lari
  • Contact 1: 0735-118-527
  • Contact 2: 0715656926
  • Contact 3: 0733422346
  • Website: kijabehospital.org

3. Kiambu Level 5 Hospital

Kiambu level 5 Hospital is top on the list of public hospitals in the county. With honourable recognition, the county government maintains the utmost standards in the healthcare facility. It is a level 5 hospital and offers both outpatient and inpatient services to clients.

  • Location: Kiambu Road Township, Kiambu
  • Contact 1: 0720 261 164
  • Contact 2: 066-2022191/739
  • Business hours: 24 hours

4. Naidu Hospital

Considered among the hospitals in Kiambu with a great ambience, Naidu Hospital is the ultimate choice for patients who appreciate nature and serenity. Besides the look and feel of this hospital, services offered at the facility are top-notch. The medical Centre accepts insurance cards and provides both outpatient and inpatient services.

  • Location: Haile Selassie Road
  • Contact 1: +254 735 600 237
  • Contact 2: +254 020 202 675

5. Kenyatta University Teaching and Referral Hospital

Famous for its state-of-the-art medical amenities, KU Referral Hospital is among the most credible hospitals in Kiambu County. The facility was officially launched in September 2020 and has a 650-bed capacity. It strives to bridge the gap to seek medical intervention abroad. The hospital also has a funeral home.

  • Location: Nothern Bypass Road, Kahawa West
  • Contact 1: + 254 780 900 519
  • Contact 2: +254 710 642 513
  • Contact 3: 254 794 589824 (WhatsApp)
  • Email: info@kutrrh.go.ke

6. Gertrude's Children’s Hospital

Gertrude’s Children Hospital is recognized for its expertise in child well-being. The facility is one of the prominent private hospitals in Kiambu County. Its acknowledgement cuts across the Eastern and Central Africa communities.

Due to its high demand, the hospital has established several branches countrywide. Its Thika branch only offers outpatient services for acute and chronic ailments in children.

  • Contacts: 0718 483 088
  • Business hours: 8:00 am- 5:00pm

7. The Nairobi Hospital

If you are hoping to run some medical checks while shopping or chilling at Kiambu Mall, The Nairobi Hospital Kiambu branch is your to-go infirmary. The hospital’s ease of access is an added advantage to outpatient clients. It is well-equipped with professionals and modern technology.

  • Location: Kiambu Mall, 2nd Floor
  • Contact: +254 701 442 277
  • Business hours: 7:00 am- 7:00 pm

8. Radiant Hospital

When seeking private hospitals in Kiambu town, you can opt for this inpatient and outpatient medical facility. It has a 24-hour emergency helpline contact, and patients are treated with the utmost care. Patients requiring special attention may book appointments with respective specialists for review and advice.

  • Location: Township, Kiambu Road
  • Contact 1: +254 717 666 626
  • Contact 2: +254 717 666 695
  • Email: info@radianthospitals.org

9. Jacaranda Health

Jacaranda Health is a haven for pregnant mothers. A facility of its kind, the facility is among level 5 hospitals in Kiambu County. The hospital is among the only three Africa facilities to be awarded a Safe Care Level 5 certification. Its reviews from existing and new clients are quite impressive.

  • Location: Kamiti Rd, Kahawa West
  • Contact 1: +254 731 999 077 (calls)
  • Contact 2: +254 786 629 763 (WhatsApp)

10. AAR Healthcare - Thika

If you are looking for private services in a medical facility, AAR Thika is an ideal choice. The hospital offers outpatient services for both adults and children. Likewise, the hospital has both general and expert practitioners who attend to various patient needs.

  • Location: Zuri Centre, Kenyatta Highway, Thika
  • Contact: +254 734 657 172/ 770/ 123/ 633
  • Email: aarthika@aar.co.ke
