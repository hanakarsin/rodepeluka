Sijal - Age, Family, Bio

Rapper and hip-hop recording artist best known for songs such as "Shol Kon" and "Nakoni Bavar." He boasts more than 1.3 million followers on his verified sijalofficial Instagram account.

Before Fame

He began releasing music professionally in 2007. His debut single is "Tabestoon Kootahe."

Trivia

He shares snapshots from his live performances and touring on Instagram. In 2022, he performed a sold out show in Istanbul. He is a member of the group Zedbazi. He also uses Spotify as a platform to release his music, and by the fall of 2022 he had seen his following on the platform grow to reach over 75,000 monthly listeners.

Family Life

He was born in Tehran, Iran.

Associated With

Both he and fellow performer Arash Labaf were born in Iran.
