Matt Goss - Age, Family, Bio

English pop singer who gained 1980s fame with the pop group Bros. He has also released multiple solo albums. His fifth album, The Beautiful Unknown, was released in March of 2022. 

Before Fame

He co-founded Bros with his brother and Craig Logan in 1986.

Trivia

He had his highest charting single with "If You Were Here Tonight" in 1996. His band Bros released only 3 studio albums throughout their career. 

Family Life

He dated Daisy Fuentes from 2001 to 2010 and has a twin brother named Luke.

Associated With

He is the brother and former bandmate of Luke Goss .
