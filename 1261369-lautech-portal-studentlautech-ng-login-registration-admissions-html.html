Ladoke Akintola University of Technology

The Ladoke Akintola University of Technology offers students opportunities for academic growth in the field of technology. The academic programs offered are from various fields of specialization such as environmental sciences, health sciences, pure and applied sciences, and engineering. These academic programs consist of pre-degree science programs, undergraduate programs, and post-graduate programs.

The university has a fully computerized modern library, making it the right choice for industry-focused and research-oriented individuals. It has two campuses which are the Osogbo and Ogbomoso campuses which have six faculties and a college. Oyo and Osun states in Nigeria own the university jointly as that is where the university is.

LAUTECH portal

The Ladoke Akintola University of Technology has a student portal created for prospective and returning students. The purpose is to ease specific academic procedures such as course registration through the LAUTECH returning students registration portal, admission list or status through the LAUTECH portal admission list and checking results through the LAUTECH result checking portal. Also, students can use it for fees payment, checking the academic calendar, extra credit hours, deferment of admission, and transcripts.

LAUTECH admission portal login

If you are a fresh student and you are looking for LAUTECH portal admission, registration, and fee payment details, you need to start by creating a portal account. Visit the official school website and click on the top of the page to create your account. Ensure that you go through the instructions on the site and understand them before creating your profile.

Click on the link, “click here to login” at the top of the web page and log in to your account. For you to access the LAUTECH application portal, you must provide a matric number and password. You can contact the university through the contact details available at the bottom of the site in case you have forgotten your password.

Once you log in successfully, ensure that you look at the information provided on the home page. You need to check out your home page because it includes relevant information such as registration dates, registration status, tuition fee payment, LAUTECH portal news, and so much more.   • Click on the link “Pay tuition” to pay your tuition fee. This payment could be part or full; therefore, you should select the applicable one. All fees are payable through the LAUTECH InterSwitch Payment Integration Platform through e-payment directly on the site. You will get a link to the inter-switch gateway during the registration process to make the payment. Ensure that you have an Interswitch enabled Debit/ ATM card that specifies the card number, expiry date, card pin, and CVV2 code. Also, ensure that you have enough money on the card to cater to your fees.
  • After you have successfully paid your tuition fees, you can now register your programs by clicking on the "Register Courses” option. Note that if you are registering during the late registration period, you will pay a late registration fee payment before you can register. You will be able to go through all the courses offered for the semester and edit your course registration form where necessary.
  • Print out your course form when you are sure about the whole process. It is important to note that you can always come back to edit your course registration if the registration period of the semester is not over.
  • Ensure that you log out every time you finish using the LAUTECH portal 2019/20. Click on “log out” before closing your browser.
