Jason Lewis - Age, Family, Bio

Supporting actor known for playing Smith Jerrod on the television series Sex and the City. He reprised his role in the Sex and the City movies. From 2017 until 2018, he played Joe Strong in the TV series Midnight, Texas.

Before Fame

He began a modeling career after graduating from San Diego State University. 

Trivia

He also had film roles in Mr. Brooks (2007) and The Jacket (2005). He has amassed over 50 on-screen roles throughout his ongoing acting career. 

Family Life

He grew up with two sisters, Katie and Nicole, and one brother named Sean. He announced his engagement to producer Liz Godwin in 2020. 

Associated With

He acted alongside Paul Walker in the 2007 action film The Death and Life of Bobby Z.
