How to pay for Netflix in Kenya

When registering your account, you will be asked to enter your credit or debit card information. The amount associated with the package selected will be automatically deducted from your account, and your subscription starts immediately.

The automatic deduction will continue in the subsequent months unless you cancel your subscription.

How to cancel your subscription

Follow the steps below to cancel your subscription to the streaming service.

  • Sign in to your account using your email address and password.
  • Click the arrow at the top right corner of the home page.
  • Click Account from the drop-down list.
  • Click Cancel Membership under the Membership & Billing section.
  • Click Finish Cancellation to confirm.

Contacts

If you need to contact the officials working for this streaming service, you can start a live chat on the website. You can also make a free call using the app from Google Play Store or App Store.

Yes, it is good for kids, as long as parents put on the kids' experience in their membership. This feature allows parents to regulate what their kids watch. A kid-friendly subscription comes with a PIN-protected parental control, allowing parents to block titles that kids should not watch.
