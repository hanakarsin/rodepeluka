Fai Khadra - Age, Family, Bio

Social star who gained a great deal of attention through his friendship with Kendall Jenner. Also a pop singer, he released a single called "Love It." He has promoted for Kendall's 818 tequila company. He has also been a model for high fashion brands like Alexander Wang. 

Before Fame

Artists that inspired him as a musician include Sam Cooke, Michael Jackson, Bob Marley, and others.

Trivia

His colorful and creative Instagram feed, called faikhadra, has earned the attention of more than 2.4 million followers. It has featured pictures with Luka Sabbat, Travis Bennett and his siblings. 

Family Life

He is the older brother of fashion icons Sama and Haya Abu Khadra. His mom, Rula, has owned a store in Riyadh called The Art of Living and his father has been an art collector.

Associated With

He dated model Devon Windsor.
