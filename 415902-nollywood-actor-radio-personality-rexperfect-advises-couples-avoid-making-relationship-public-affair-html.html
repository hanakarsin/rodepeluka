Speaking in an exclusive interview with TUKO.co.ke, Rexperfect said:

"Marry someone you see as a friend, someone you can discuss almost everything with. Before allowing a third party, make sure you have exhausted every means of reconciliation."

Rexperfect further said marriage is not for the faint-hearted, giving tips for a healthy relationship.

"Never walk away on your partner except at the point of anger (to avoid domestic violence or utterances that could haunt the future."

He also said that comparing your partner to your exes was disrespectful.

"Never compare your partner to your ex or anyone else. Be content. Work on it to befit your taste," he advised.

Adding:

"For a relationship to work, there must be a commitment from both parties. Do not hold back your love for one another."
