Rudeboy celebrates as Reason With Me becomes 2019's most viewed African video on YouTube

- Rudeboy has taken to social media to celebrate the success of one of his songs on YouTube

- The singer revealed that his Reason With Me music video has received the highest views for an African video in 2019

- Reason With Me music video has 40 million views on YouTube

Popular Nigerian singer and former member of the disbanded duo group P-Square, Paul Okoye, now known professionally as Rudeboy, has taken to social media to celebrate the success of one of his songs on YouTube.

The singer shared a post on his Instagram page to celebrate after his music video took the top spot for most video African music videos on YouTube.

Rudeboy said his song Reason With Me became the most viewed video on the platform with 40 million views.
