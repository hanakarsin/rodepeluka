Tae Caldwell - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube personality and broadcaster who rose to fame as a member of the popular challenge, prank, and story time channel Jazz and Tae alongside her former girlfriend Jazmine Hood. The channel has amassed more than 3.9 million subscribers. She has also gained over 1.1 million followers on her tae_caldwell Instagram where she has posted multiple photos wearing the brand Fashion Nova. 

Before Fame

She launched her channel in 2013.

Trivia

In late 2017, she posted her first mirror selfie on her tae_caldwell Instagram account. She also owns her own beauty company called Bougie Bad Lashes. 

Family Life

She is from Houston, Texas. She has a brother named Jalyn. 

Associated With

She frequently quoted celebrities such as Beyonce.
