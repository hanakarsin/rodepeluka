Ruto's warm birthday message to Moses Kuria: &quot;Thanks for being hustler movement champion&quot;

- Ruto said it was a great blessing to have a politician of Kuria's caliber around him as he strategises to take first jab at the presidency

Deputy President William Ruto has said he is lucky to have Gatundu South legislator Moses Kuria in his camp.

Ruto in his birthday message to Kuria, described the Gatundu politician as a hustler movement champion who will be instrumental as he strategises to take first jab at the presidency in 2022.

"We celebrate you mheshimiwa Moses Kuria as you turn 50. It’s a blessing to have you as a friend and one of the champions of the hustler movement. Happy birthday," he said.

On his part, Kuria assured the deputy president of his full support promising not to betray and dump him at the end of the road when he will need his services most.

"Thank you my boss. I remain loyal and not treacherous like others," he responded.

Kuria recently sparked a debate on social media after using a riddle to describe President Uhuru's conduct ahead of his retirement in 2022.

Lately, Uhuru has been lashing out at his deputy Ruto for criticising Jubilee government, the same administration he serves as the DP.

In one of the recent remarks, Uhuru said he will not hand over power "to a thief" who will steal from Kenyans.

On Friday, February 12, the president sustained his onslaught on Ruto and asked him to resign if he was unhappy with the way the government was being run.
