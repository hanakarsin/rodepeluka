Carrefour Kenya: owner, offers, branches, contacts

Carrefour Kenya is among the largest and fastest-growing hypermarkets in the country. It deals with food, no-food products, and services. Since its establishment in 2016, it has been working towards satisfying the needs of its customers as well as filling the gap that was left by the exit of two giant Kenyan supermarkets; Nakumatt and Uchumi. What else do you know about the retail store?

Over time, the French multinational store has proven worthy in the region's competitive market. Its annual profits have been growing yearly. For instance, in 2018, its sales amounted to Sh13.87 billion, which is an increment of Sh8.11 from the previous year. The increment can be attributed to its vast expansion that is aimed at reaching a broad customer base.

Essential details about Carrefour Kenya

There is a lot you do not know about the giant chain store. For instance, who is the owner of Carrefour? How many branches are there in the country? Are they available in your local area? Do they have offers?

Who owns Carrefour Kenya?

The giant retail store is under the ownership of Majid Al Futtaim. He founded the business in 1992 but opened the first store in Kenya in 2016.

Apart from being responsible for the establishment of the franchise in the country, he is also in charge of the same business in other parts of the world, such as Asia and the Middle East. Thanks to his impeccable marketing strategies, he has entirely transformed the retail market in the mentioned regions.
