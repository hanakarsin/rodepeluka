March 22 Horoscope and Zodiac

Although choosing a career is one of life's greatest challenges, you are fortunate enough to have natural abilities that can translate into success in a number of fields. Your determination is perfect for competitive careers, such as sales, business, advertising, or marketing. Similarly, your passion could allow you to be a pioneer in any field you choose, spanning from science to engineering. If you are musically inclined, your vision could make you a successful composer or lyricist, which was the case for Andrew Lloyd Webber, who was also born on March 22nd. If the entertainment world is appealing, look to the accomplishments of Reese Witherspoon, another of your celebrity birthday twins, for motivation.
