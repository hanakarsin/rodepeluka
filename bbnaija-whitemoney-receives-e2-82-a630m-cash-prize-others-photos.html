Whitemoney Receives 30m Cash Prize, Others (Photos)

The organizers of Big Brother Naija on Monday presented 30 million Naira to the winner of the ‘Shine Ya Eye’ edition, Whitemoney.

The prizes were presented to Whitemoney live on the BBNaija channel on DSTV on Monday.

The reality tv star was also presented with a house key and a car by the organizers of the highly watched show.

Among the prizes, Whitemoney got on Monday was a ₦30m cash prize, cash in an Abeg digital wallet and bitcoins courtesy of Patricia, a two-bedroom apartment courtesy of RevolutionPlus Property, a top of range SUV from Innoson Motors, and a sponsored trip for two.

Whitemoney emerged the winner on Sunday during the last eviction show after defeating Liquorose, Pere, Cross, Angel and Emmanuel.

The businessman had 47% votes to clinch the grand prize.
