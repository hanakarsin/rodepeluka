Dylan Lee - Age, Family, Bio

Model, musician and social media star known as the muse of designer Yves Saint Laurent. He is also well recognized as the son of Baywatch star Pamela Anderson and Motley Crue drummer Tommy Lee. 

Before Fame

In 2002, he appeared alongside the rest of his family in the documentary Playboy: The Ultimate Pamela Anderson.

Trivia

Extremely popular on Instagram, he has accumulated a following of more than 170,000 fans on the photo sharing app.

Family Life

He has one brother named Brandon Thomas. His aunt, Athena Lee, is a popular musician.

Associated With

His uncle, James Kottak, is recognized as the drummer for the German rock band the Scorpions.
