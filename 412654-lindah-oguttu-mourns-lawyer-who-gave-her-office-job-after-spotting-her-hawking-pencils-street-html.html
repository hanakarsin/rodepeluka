Lindah Oguttu Pays Tribute to Lawyer Who Gave Her Office Job after Spotting Her Hawking Pencils on S

- TV journalist Lindah Oguttu mourned her lawyer friend who helped her get a job

- According to the scribe, the lawyer found her hawking before offering her an opportunity in his office that helped her manage to pay for her college tuition

- Her fans sent their condolences with some saying people should say such messages to their loved ones when they are still alive

TV news anchor Lindah Oguttu has shared a touching post on Twitter paying tribute to the lawyer friend who helped her become who she is today.

The beautiful journalist disclosed that the deceased legal practitioner found her selling pencils outside a busy building in the town centre and helped her get a job in his office.

This is what she shared on Twitter:

"Lawyer Joseph Wagara, he made me. I met him while hawking pencils outside International Life House. Pushed me into a great path in life. I am broken. Rest in peace, my friend. I am because of you.
