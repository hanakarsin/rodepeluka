PrestonPlayz - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube gaming phenomenon best known for his channels TBNRfrags, Preston, PrestonGamez, and PrestonPlayz in which he frequently posts videos featuring the games Fortnite, Minecraft and Among Us. His Preston channel has amassed over 27 million subscribers and he also has over 10 million subscribers on his PrestonPlayz channel. His full name is Preston Arsement. 

Before Fame

He launched TBNRfrags in November of 2010 and PrestonPlayz in July of 2012.

Trivia

He plays with and against his friends Noochm, BajanCanadian, and JeromeASF. His first YouTube video to reach 50 million views was a video from January 2019 where he pranked his younger half-brother Joshua's Minecraft house.  Many of his most popular videos are about Roblox.

Family Life

He has two younger half brothers named Joshua and Caleb and a younger half-sister, Keeley, who all play Minecraft. He also has an older brother named Daka who served in the Navy and his father is named David. He married Brianna Paige Arsement in May of 2018. He often features his brother-in-law in his channel. 

Associated With

He and Mitch Hughes, AKA BajanCanadian, help each other when they compete in Battle-Dome.
