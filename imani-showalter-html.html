Imani Showalter - Age, Family, Bio

Singer known as the former fiancé of NBA star Stephen Jackson. She and Jackson were a couple for more than a decade and had two children together. She appeared on season one of VH1's Basketball Wives LA.  

Before Fame

She was a member of a New York-based girl group. 

Trivia

Stephen Jackson said he called off their nuptials on their wedding day in 2005 because she refused to sign a prenuptial agreement. She refuted his claims on Instagram, saying he was told two weeks before the wedding that the agreement hadn't been signed. 

Family Life

In 2012, there were reports she was dating gospel star Marvin Sapp. The singer denied the rumors. Her ex fiancé went on to marry Renata Jackson in 2009, but they separated in 2015.

Associated With

Her Basketball Wives LA costars included Gloria Govan and Malaysia Pargo.
