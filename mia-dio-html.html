Mia Dio - Age, Family, Bio

Social star best known for her miadio TikTok account, where she primarily posts comedy skits and makeup and beauty content. She has over 5 million fans on the platform. She is also popular on YouTube where she has posted travel vlogs, story time videos, makeup tutorials, challenges, and more. 

Before Fame

Before gaining popularity on YouTube, she played varsity soccer and modeled in the music video "Sin Tus Besos." 

Trivia

Fluent in Spanish, she also has a Spanish YouTube channel in addition to her main channel. 

Family Life

Born to Claudia and Luis Dio, she has four brothers and one sister. Her biological father is from Chile, but she was raised by an Argentine father and a Brazilian mother. 

Associated With

Often associated with Ethan and Grayson Dolan, the twins appeared in her first YouTube video, where she met them for the first time. She has appeared in videos with Nate Garner.
