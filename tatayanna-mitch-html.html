Tatayanna Mitchell - Age, Family, Bio

Comedy, dance, lip-sync and POV creator best known for her therealtati TikTok account. She has gone on to garner more than 5 million followers on the platform. She often does duets on TikTok and is known to participate in acting challenges with her collaborators.   

Before Fame

She made a duet featuring Charli D'Amelio early in her career.

Trivia

She has a YouTube channel called Therealtati where she has published food reviews and mukbang content. She is a member of the House of Us TikTok collective which aims to inspire Gen Z to exercise their right to vote.

Family Life

She was born in Detroit, Michigan. She has a sister named Mae. 

Associated With

She is widely known for her collaborations with Devin Caherly on TikTok.
