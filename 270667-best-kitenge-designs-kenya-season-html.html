Best kitenge designs in Kenya this season

Derived from the Swahili word Kitengele, the Kitenge, also known as Chitenge, is popularly worn in East Africa and other parts of Africa. Initially, women used the Kitenge as a wrap and a baby carrier. But as time went by, the fabric usually printed in multiple colors and patterns became a favorite for people attending ceremonies such as weddings. The Kitenge holds some significance in the different cultures in Kenya. Even when the country was deliberating on national attire, Kitenge dress designs dominated the suggestions.
