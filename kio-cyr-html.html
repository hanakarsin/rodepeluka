Kio Cyr - Age, Family, Bio

Video creator on TikTok who posts lip-syncs, transitions, trends, challenges and other entertaining videos to his kiocyrrr account, which has earned over 9 million fans. His videos regularly receive over a million views each. He earned over 10 million views on an October 2021 video in which he did a push-up with his girlfriend Amanda Díaz on his back. 

Before Fame

The first TikTok video on his account was him dancing on the roof of his house in celebration of high school graduation being 2 weeks away.

Trivia

He was a competitive soccer player. He has earned more than 2.2 million followers for his kiocyr Instagram account. He earned representation with TalentX Entertainment. 

Family Life

The oldest picture on his Instagram was a Mother's Day post. His mom is Thai and his dad is French Canadian. He began a relationship with Olivia Ponton in 2020, but the couple broke up later in the same year. He subsequently began dating Amanda Díaz.

Associated With

In 2019, he joined Griffin Johnson, Bryce Hall and other popular influencers as a member of the social group Sway House.
