Tonto Dikeh Advises Nigerians On Using 'God When' On Social Media

Popular Nollywood actress, Tonto Dikeh has decided to send an advice to her fans. The advice is coming after leaked audio and chat of her conversation with her ex-boyfriend, Prince Kpokpogri hit social media.

The post was made via her Instagram page and she pleaded with people to be careful of what they do “God when” to because they do not have all the information and only see what they are allowed to see.

“God when” is a phrase used to imply when God would do the same thing he has done for that particular person. It is often used in social media to show people are tapping into a blessing.

Naija News recalls that after the leaked audio and tape which had led to most Nigerians condemning Kpokpogri for releasing a private conversation he had with his ex lover and praising Tonto for her silence, Kpokpogri had denied that he was responsible for the leaked tape.

He had also posted a message sent by Tonto that was threatening to deal with him behind the scene.
