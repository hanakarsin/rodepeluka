Markian Tarasiuk - Age, Family, Bio

Vancouver-based actor who got his start acting on the professional stage throughout Winnipeg. He made his film debut in Bunks in 2013 and has gone on to appear in iZombie and A Time to Dance. 

Before Fame

He studied at Vancouver's Studio 58 acting school.

Trivia

He is a fan of the Toronto Blue Jays.

Family Life

Often posts with his girlfriend Lauren Jackson.

Associated With

He played the role of Randle Patrick McMurphy in a stage production of One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest, a role made famous by Jack Nicholson.
