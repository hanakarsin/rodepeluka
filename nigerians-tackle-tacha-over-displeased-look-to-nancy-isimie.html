Nigerians Tackle BBNaija's Tacha Over 'Displeased Look' At Actress Nancy Isimie

Nigerians have tackled former Big Brother Naija Housemate Tacha over a displeased look at Nollywood actress Nancy Isimie.

Nancy Isimie is seen wearing a short colourful skirt, white top and hat to compliment her outfit.
