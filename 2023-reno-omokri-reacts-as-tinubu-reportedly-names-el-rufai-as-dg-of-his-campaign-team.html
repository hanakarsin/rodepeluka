Reno Omokri Reacts As Tinubu Reportedly Names El-Rufai As DG Of His Campaign Team

Former Presidential aide, Reno Omokri has reacted to reports that the Kaduna State Governor, Nasir El-Rufai has been named the Director-General of the campaign council of the All Progressives Congress (APC) presidential candidate, Bola Tinubu.

According to Dailytrust, a source close to the leadership of APC hinted that former national chairman of the party and senatorial aspirant, Adams Oshiomhole was named as chairman of Tinubu’s presidential campaign council which is yet to be made official.

Similarly, the former Lagos State governor also appointed El-Rufai, as the Director-General of the campaign council.

In a post shared on Twitter, Reno described the move as a subtle jihad against Christians, questioning if he cannot find a Christain to be the DG of his campaign.

He wrote: “Tinubu named El-Rufai as the DG of his campaign? Wow! This is a subtle Jihad against Christians?

Presidential candidate-Muslim
Running mate-Muslim
Now DG is also-Muslim

“You mean that of the many Christian Governors in the APC, Tinubu could not find a Christian to be the DG of his campaign? Not even one? He had to pick Nasir El-Rufai, a notorious enemy of the Body of Christ, as his DG? Perhaps it is not a coincidence that Naira is ₦666 to $1 today! Is that a sign?

“The only time they needed Christians was for the Fake Bishops show they gave to Nigerians.”

Bola Tinubu, Shettima Meet El-Rufai, Matawalle, Oshiomhole, Others

Tinubu, and his running mate, Kashim Shettima met some party chieftains regarding the forthcoming general elections.

Naija News reports that the meeting was held on Wednesday at the residence of the APC presidential flagbearer in Abuja, the nation’s capital.

Some of the critical APC stakeholders present at the meeting were Governor Nasir El-Rufai of Kaduna State, Governor Bello Matawalle of Zamfara state and Simon Lalong of Plateau state.

Others present at the meeting included the former APC National Chairman, Adams Oshiomhole; APC National Secretary, Senator Iyiola Omisore and the former running mate to Tinubu, Ibrahim Masari.
