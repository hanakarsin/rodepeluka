Anna Louise (YouTube Star) - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube vlogger best known as a Monday vlogger for the SevenPerfectAngels channel, a sub-channel of the SevenAwesomeKids network. She would leave the growingly successful collaborative channel in 2017.

Before Fame

She uploaded an audition video for SevenAwesomeKids in May of 2014 on her AnnasFabulousFilms channel. 

Trivia

One of her most watched videos on the SevenPerfectAngels channel is called "Anna's Morning Routine." 

Family Life

Her older sister Avery shares the Monday spot with her on the SevenPerfectAngels YouTube channel. She has a Cavalier King Charles Spaniel named Gracie who has appeared in her videos.

Associated With

She and Nicole Luellen were both selected to be a part of the SevenPerfectAngels channel.
