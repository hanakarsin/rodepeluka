Hale Leon - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram star, social media influencer, and model who is known for sharing a blend of lifestyle, relationship, and traveling-based content through his account for his over 100,000 followers. He gained further attention when it was revealed he was dating Big Brother houseguest Frankie Grande. 

Before Fame

He first started sharing his modeling and lifestyle based content through Instagram in October of 2013. He then gained further attention in April of 2019 when it was revealed that he was dating Frankie Grande. 

Trivia

In addition to his modeling and social media work, he is also known for his passion for video games and works as an App Developer. In 2021, he posted a popular Halloween post that featured his Venom costume. 

Family Life

He was born and raised in New York and shared a photo with both his sister and nephew to Instagram in January of 2019. He married Frankie Grande on May 4th, 2022. 

Associated With

He is married to Big Brother star Frankie Grande who is the brother of pop singer and musical icon Ariana Grande.
