Mike Sonko's daughter cautions women against staying in unhappy long-term relationships

On Thursday, September 26, word hit the street that gorgeous US musician Cassie Ventura got married to her lover Alex Fine.

Nearly everyone around the world was excited given that the beauty had previously been in an 11-year relationship with rapper Diddy without ever getting an engagement ring.

An elated Saumu Mbuvi who also happens to be Nairobi Governor Mike Sonko’s daughter also decided to chime in on the issue.

According to the mother of one, women should avoid settling when it comes to relationships.

To her, any moment a lady senses that a man could be toxic, she is required to pack her bags and hit the road.

Saumu added that sitting and hoping an abusive man will change is a waste of time.

“Word for today. Cassie dated Diddy for 11 years, he never proposed or had any plans to marry her or have a family with her,” the single mama said.

The yummy mummy then added that women need to learn how to fall for men who appreciate them.

“Stop being in relationships where you are not appreciated. Your happiness should always come first. A man who loves you will do anything to be with you. Tomorrow is not promised why spend your life being miserable?” she posed.

Saumu then asked women to avoid forcing themselves on men who no longer have feelings for them or shower them with love.
