Safaricom beats Telkom, Airtel in customer experience new survey

- A new survey by Ajua has ranked Safaricom top in customer experience with a net promoter score (NPS) of 31

- Airtel and Telkom emerged joint second with a NPS score of 28

- Safaricom's position was attributed to great internet offers and promotions like storo bonus, dabodabo and tunukiwa among others

Telecommunications market leader Safaricom has emerged top in customer experience beating Telkom and Airtel to second place, a new survey has revealed.

The third quarter customer loyalty industry benchmark by integrated customer experience company Ajua (formerly msurvey) showed Safaricom had a net promoter score of 31 from 27 while both Telkom and Airtel scored 28.

Safaricoms’ position was attributed to great internet offers and promotions like storo bonus, dabodabo and tunukiwa among others.

“Safaricom has great 4G capability, great promotional materials e.g. 200% bonus and great tariffs,” one subscriber quoted.

The survey noted there were few complaints of Safaricom customers’ whining about fast depletion of internet bundles and airtime over the last three months.

Airtel and Telkom were however, hailed for offering cheaper call rates and amazing data bundles.

“The Telkom internet is just so perfect, they have offers like free calls as well as free internet options,” one Telkom subscriber said.

On the other hand, Airtel customers cited unstable network connectivity in some areas.

“Their services are excellent, talk of good call rates, promotions, affordable data bundles. The only problem is network connection in some areas,” one customer bemoaned.

The survey found network (26%), price (16%) and customer service (12%) as the main drivers of customer experience.

Others were speed/efficiency (12%), bundles and internet offers (11%), products (7%) and convenience (4%) among others.

In the last survey done by the same firm two months ago, Telkom beat Safaricom in the same category with a NPS score of 38 to 32.

At that time respondents attributed their high score award for Telkom on what they termed as affordable data and call offers.
