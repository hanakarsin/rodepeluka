My dead wife was brought back to life with cloth anointed by Adeboye - RCCG pastor

- A pastor at RCCG has narrated how his dead wife was brought back to life with a cloth anointed by Pastor Adeboye

- He noticed the woman had died during an early morning private prayer session in their bedroom, after she did not respond to the prayers

- Just as they were rushing her to the hospital, the pastor stated that he wrapped her with the anointed cloth, and the corpse came back to life shortly afterwards

A pastor of the Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) has testified about how he used a dress anointed by the general overseer of the church, Pastor Enoch Adeboye, to raise his wife from the dead.

According to Premium Times, the testimony was shared during the church’s Special Holy Ghost Service.

Legit.ng gathers that the general overseer had anointed clothes worn by people during the March Holy Ghost Service, and advised that they be used for miracles during difficult cases.

The pastor, Timothy Owoade, had reportedly woken up early one morning last month, to pray with his wife before they joined the kids for larger family prayers.

He stated that he noticed that his wife did not respond to the prayers; so he tapped her to make sure she was okay. According to the pastor, after a quick examination, he noticed that she was dead, so he raised an alarm to alert the kids and neighbors.

However, he stated that as they were taking her to the hospital, he remembered the anointed dress and rushed to get it, and then wrapped it around the corpse.

He stated that before getting to the hospital, the wife suddenly came back to life.
