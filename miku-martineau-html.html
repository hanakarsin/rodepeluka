Miku Martineau - Age, Family, Bio

Actress known for her roles in Kate, Ruby and the Well and Finny the Shark. Her miku.martineau Instagram account features photos from events, travels and milestones. She also posts promotional info for current and upcoming projects. She has attracted 200,000 followers. 

Before Fame

When she was 12, she landed a part as a voice actor in the animated series Carl's Car Wash. 

Trivia

She is interested in photography. 

Family Life

She is the daughter of Peter and Kumiko Martineau. Her brother is Kai Martineau. Her mother is Japanese. 

Associated With

Mary Elizabeth Winstead also starred in Kate.
