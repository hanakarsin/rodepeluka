Maddie Gray (Instagram Star) - Age, Family, Bio

Instagram star and model who is best recognized for sharing fitness related photos and other modeling content with her over 110,000 followers. She is a Bang Energy ambassador and is also known for sharing both gym and at home exercise videos through her account as well. 

Before Fame

She first started sharing lifestyle based photos through her Instagram in June 2012. She expanded to more fitness-related content in June 2015. 

Trivia

She is also known for being a certified On The Go fitness trainer. 

Family Life

She is also known for having dated Connor Kimball and shared a photo of them together through her Instagram in January of 2018. She is now dating a man named Robert Francis.

Associated With

She shared a photo with rapper TI in Atlanta through her Instagram in April of 2019.
