Njoro: Police Officer Storms Hospital, Shoots Girlfriend Before Turning Gun on Himself

  • Constable Bernard Sivo shot and killed Mary Nyambura at Njoro Sub-County Hospital where she had sought treatment
  • Nyambura had suffered a fractured leg after being assaulted by Sivo
  • The couple's bodies were moved to Egerton University Morgue awaiting postmortem

Police officers are investigating an incident where their colleague based at Njoro Police Station shot and killed his girlfriend before turning a gun on himself.

The officer, Constable Bernard Sivo, is said to have attacked Mary Nyambura at Njoro Sub-County Hospital where she had sought treatment after suffering a fractured leg.

According to a police report seen by TUKO.co.ke, the couple initially had an argument in their house that culminated in Sivo aged 28 assaulting her and breaking her right leg.

The 29-year-old later managed to go to the hospital, and it was while she was being treated when the officer emerged armed with an AK-47 rifle.

Sivo, in turn, pumped several bullets in her killing her on the spot. This prompted the medics who were attending to her to scamper for their safety.

The officer then came back to the station while wild and was seen firing aimlessly within the station endangering the lives of other fellow officers.

Police were mobilized to disarm him but unfortunately, he shot himself on the chin and the bullet exited on the upper forehead and died instantly.

At the scene, police recovered an AK 47 rifle with a magazine of 11 rounds of 7.62mm.

While at the scene the deceased body was found having been shot severally on the right arm, right wrist and on her right back

The bodies were moved to Egerton University morgue awaiting postmortem.
