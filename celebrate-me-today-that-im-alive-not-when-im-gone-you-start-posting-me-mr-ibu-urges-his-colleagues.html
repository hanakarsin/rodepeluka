'Celebrate Me Today That I'm Alive Not When I'm Gone You Start Posting Me'

Comic Nollywood actor, John Okafor, popularly known as Mr Ibu, has called on his colleagues to celebrate him.

Naija News reports that Ibu made the call amidst the usual trend where celebrities put up photos of their colleague after death but rarely celebrate their birthday.

The comic actor took to his Instagram page to share a video of himself dancing to the hit song ‘Cough’ by Nigerian singer, Kizz Daniel.

Mr Ibu urged his colleague to celebrate him today when he is alive instead of posting his pictures with so many compliments when he is dead.

The actor also challenged them to repost the video as he would want to see the number of people who really love him.

He wrote: “Celebrate me Today that am alive not when am gone you start posting me up and down with so many compliments! Let’s see how many people will share this video today! Can we get to a million followers here today? Let’s see who really loves me!!!”

My Christian Album Would Be Played In The Club

Meanwhile, popular Nigerian singer, Ayodeji Balogun also known as Wizkid, has opened up on his latest album MMLE, which was released today.

Wizkid during a recent interview on an international radio was asked a couple of questions.

The singer, while speaking about the kind of music he creates, stated that every album he makes will always be vibe-on vibes.

He further stated that if he does make a “Christian album today, you are going to play it in the club.”

The singer answered a couple of questions, including the one where he was asked to pick his preference between Tems and Burna Boy.

To which he replied that they are both families.
