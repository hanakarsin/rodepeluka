Nigerian Governor, Convoy Trapped In Heavy Flood

The Edo State Governor, Godwin Obaseki and his official convoy reportedly got trapped in a heavy flood after a rainfall in the state.

Naija News reports that in a video making the rounds online, a resident of the state was heard making a mockery of the governor and his team.

The video also showed the governor’s convoy struggling to move away from the flooded road.

The man who made a jest of Obaseki said the administration has refused to construct good roads in the state and has received a share of what the masses are suffering.

According to him, the traffic on Benin Sapele Road at the RCC axis of the road was caused by bad portions of the road, and several calls had been made for the road to be fixed but they have all been ignored by the authorities.

Couple, Two Children, In-Law Die Of Generator Fumes

In other news, in what could be described as tragic, a couple, their two children and others have reportedly died of generator fumes in Anambra State.

Naija News learnt that the incident happened at their residence in the Nkwele Ezunaka area of Onitsha.

It was gathered that the family and an apprentice inhaled the poisonous fumes from a generator on Wednesday, which eventually took their lives.

Reports claimed that the man’s wife had just delivered a baby, and to give the wife a surprise gift, he arranged for the family to move to their new house.

Sources, according to The PUNCH, confirmed that it was the family’s first night in their new residence, adding that only the four-day-old baby survived the tragic incident.
