Bugha - Age, Family, Bio

Professional e-sports competitor who is recognized for having been the winner of the 2019 Fortnite World Cup championship. He was a member of the Sentinels e-sports organization and also live streamed on Twitch where he amassed more than 4.9 million followers.

Before Fame

He competed in Epic Games' World Cup Warmup in 2019. 

Trivia

During game one of the 2019 Fortnite World Cup he clocked nine eliminations and the Victory Royale. He won Esports PC Player of the Year in 2019 at the Esports Awards.

Family Life

He was born Kyle Giersdorf and lived in the United States with his parents. He's been based in Pennsylvania. He had a girlfriend named Angelica whom he started dating in May 2019.

Associated With

Both he and Tfue competed in the solo division during the 2019 Fortnite World Cup championship.
