Jazzy Le - Age, Family, Bio

YouTube content creator of DIY, beauty, lifestyle and hair videos who posts to a self-titled channel. Her YouTube videos focus on outfit assembly, shopping hauls, and inside looks at her daily routine and personal life. 

Before Fame

She loves to dance and went to dance competitions regularly. She created her YouTube channel in July of 2013 under the name jazzylebeauty, but her first public video wasn't posted until June of 2014.

Trivia

She reached over 700,000 subscribers. Her jasminele Instagram page has also become popular, with a following of over 200,000 fans. 

Family Life

Her sister Amber Le also has a YouTube channel. Her first name is Jasmine.

Associated With

She ran into fellow YouTuber Eva Gutowski at Disneyland.
