Funke Akindele, Omoni Oboli, Mercy Johnson Gush Over AY's Daughter's Photo As They Storm Church For

Nigerian comedian cum filmmaker Ayo Makun popularly called AY has shared a beautiful photo of his second daughter Ayomide as they storm the church for their thanksgiving.

Recall AY and his wife Mabel Makun welcomed a baby girl earlier this year after 13 years of having their first daughter and the feeling has been overwhelming.

A video shared on his Instagram page captured AY, his wife and his first daughter in church as they dance in excitement.

Unarguably the joy of AY and his wife welcoming a child after 13 years is unending.

In a subsequent post unveiling the face of his daughter, AY said welcoming his second child after 13 years has been a blessing to him and his wife.

He wrote: “Welcoming AYOMIDE into our world after 13 years, has been a blessing to @realmabelmakun and myself. We are ready to take our baby to church tomorrow before God. And our intention is to raise AYOMIDE under his mercy and special grace”.

“Our baby is a gift from God. We shall dedicate her to the same God in church before His people, in appreciation of His love and kindness towards us as a family unit”
