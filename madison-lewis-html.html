Mads Lewis - Age, Family, Bio

Content creator and actress who has earned over 12 million fans on TikTok posting lips syncs and viral dance videos. Her TikTok handle is mads.yo. She portrays Birdie Kaye on the popular web series Chicken Girls. She has been a member of the Just A House content collective. She has a merchandise line through Fanjoy that includes many items with her branded JRYB slogan, which stands for "Just Remember You're Beautiful." 

Before Fame

She found a following on TikTok when the app was known as musical.ly. 

Trivia

She has earned over 6 million followers on Instagram posting pictures from her daily life. She has a self-titled YouTube channel where she has posted a range of videos including makeup videos and challenges. 

Family Life

She is a triplet with her sister Riley and her brother Dakota. She lives in California. She dated Jaden Hossler. She also dated Charles Gitnick from July 2018 to September 2019. She was previously romantically linked to fellow TikTok star Christian Plourde.

Associated With

She stars alongside Jules LeBlanc and Hayden Summerall in Chicken Girls.
